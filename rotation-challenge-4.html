Set up the round

Players see one source block and must mentally rotate it. Distractors include one almost-correct rotated version plus other wrong turns.

How it plays

  • Look at the source shape carefully.
  • Mental task: rotate it 90 degrees clockwise around the vertical axis.
  • Pick the matching option from the answer bank.

Tip: watch where the tallest stacks move after rotation. Front corners become side corners.
